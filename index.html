Welcome to Dehli Public School Ghaziabad Vasundhara

Delhi Public School Ghaziabad was conceived and founded by Mr. Om Pathak, the then District Magistrate of Ghaziabad in 1980 with support from Sh. Dharamvira and Lt. Colonel Dr. GPS Waraich, the then Principal of DPS, Mathura Road.                                                                                     Delhi Public School Ghaziabad Vasundhara, a learning school, formed on May 3, 1999, at SBC Plaza, sector 15. It shifted to its present locale, Sector 9 Vasundhara on March 29, 2000, and has, since, carved a niche for itself in the society.                                                                                       DPSG Vasundhara with its comprehensive vision of a 'happy school with a blend of values, tradition, technology and a quest for excellence' stands committed to imparting quality education for the holistic development of the children.
